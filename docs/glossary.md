# Business Glossary

## active_subscriber

A subscriber with an active service relationship during a defined reporting period. Typical rules include an active account status, at least one provisioned service, and no completed disconnect before the reporting date.

## arpu

Average revenue per user. ARPU is commonly calculated as eligible recurring and usage revenue divided by the average subscriber count for the period.

## churn

A customer or subscriber disconnect event. Churn metrics should specify whether they measure voluntary churn, involuntary churn, gross churn, net churn, account churn, or line-level churn.

## data_freshness

The age of a dataset relative to its expected update schedule. Freshness is often measured by source modification time, ingestion time, or the maximum business event timestamp.

## lineage

The relationship between source datasets, transformations, and downstream catalog assets. Lineage helps users understand where data came from and what could be affected by a change.
